## Description (paste into the page body)

You don't fight. You *lead*. Your critters orbit you as a living shield —
send them out one at a time to hunt, call them home to guard you, and
balance attack against defense in real time with just two buttons.

🐰 **12 critters** that merge into KINGS — bunnies, frogs, a moose, a
wizard mouse — every one of them huggable
🎼 **4 bandleaders** with completely different rhythms (the drummer sends
THREE at once; the conductor's recalls explode)
🗺️ **7 arenas** with real strategy: robots short-circuit in ponds, mud
slows everyone but frogs, lightning storms strike the robots too
🤖 **3 giant bosses**, ending with BUNNYTRON, the colossal pink robo-bunny
🌰 **A whole economy**: drafts, loadouts, a between-wave market, a
training camp, quests, spice-jar difficulty dials, endless mode
👨‍👧‍👦 **Built WITH kids, FOR families**: a voice announces everything for
pre-readers, losses are always explained, co-op is drop-in, and there are
no guns — the mob IS the weapon

Made by a dad and playtested (ruthlessly) by his kids.

**Controls:** WASD + Space (send) + Shift (recall). Second player: arrow
keys + Enter, or a controller. That's the whole game.
